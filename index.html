Our Programs

Secondary School

Admission: JSS1 - SSS3

Our secondary school offers a challenging academic program designed to prepare students for national and international examinations. With a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership, we equip students to succeed in higher education and beyond.

Primary School

Admission: Primary 1 - Primary 5

The primary school program encourages curiosity and fosters academic growth. Through an engaging curriculum that blends core subjects with arts, sports, and technology, we ensure that every child gains a strong academic foundation while developing key life skills.

Nursery

Admission: Day Care - Nursery 2

Our nursery program provides a nurturing and playful environment where young children explore, grow, and develop essential skills. We focus on early literacy, numeracy, and social interaction to prepare them for primary education.
